================================================================================
JOB SUMMARY
================================================================================

The Data and Intelligence Directorate is looking for a Senior Data Scientist to
lead the development of predictive and risk models that support transport safety
and enforcement decisions across Great Britain.

You will design, build and maintain analytical models that help allocate frontline
resources more effectively, identify emerging risks, and drive evidence-based
policy. You will work closely with operational teams, policy colleagues and
external partners to ensure your analysis has real-world impact.

================================================================================
JOB DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

Your responsibilities will include:

- Developing and maintaining predictive models for transport safety risk assessment.
- Using machine learning and statistical methods to identify patterns in large
  operational datasets.
- Designing and implementing data pipelines to support reproducible analysis.
- Presenting findings and recommendations to senior leaders and operational staff.
- Championing best practice in data science across the directorate.
- Mentoring junior analysts and data scientists.
- Evaluating the performance of models and improving them iteratively.
- Ensuring ethical and responsible use of data throughout your work.
- Collaborating with engineers and analysts to deploy models into production.

================================================================================
PERSON SPECIFICATION — REQUIRED EXPERIENCE
================================================================================

- Developing and deploying machine learning models to solve operational problems.
- Building reproducible data pipelines using Python or similar languages.
- Working with large or complex datasets, including geospatial or time-series data.
- Communicating analytical findings to non-technical stakeholders.
- Experience of cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP or equivalent).
- Promoting professional development and sharing best practice with colleagues.
- Applying ethics frameworks and data protection principles in analytical work.
- Strong programming skills in Python, including data science libraries.
- Experience with version control, testing, and collaborative development practices.
- Working in Agile or iterative delivery environments.
